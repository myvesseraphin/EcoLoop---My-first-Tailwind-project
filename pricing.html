Common Inquiries

Everything you need to know about the EcoLoop ecosystem.

How accurate is the AI recognition?

Our neural network is trained on over 2 million waste samples, achieving a **98.4% accuracy rate** in multi-waste environments. It can distinguish between different types of polymers (plastics), metals, and even specific electronic components.

How do I get paid for my waste?

Once the AI classifies your waste, EcoLoop calculates its "Worth" based on real-time market commodities. You can list your items on our marketplace, where certified recyclers bid on your materials. Funds are transferred instantly to your digital wallet upon pickup.

Is the IoT hardware required?

No. While our **Smart-Bin IoT kit** automates the process for Professional and Enterprise users, Starter users can use our mobile app to scan and log waste manually using their smartphone camera.

How does EcoLoop support SDGs?

We directly contribute to **SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities)** and **SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption)**. By digitizing the circular economy, we provide the data transparency needed to reduce landfill reliance and carbon footprints.
