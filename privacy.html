01 Our Commitment

At EcoLoop, we believe your data should be as circular as your waste. We collect only what is necessary to power our AI Vision and Marketplace tools. Unlike traditional tech platforms, we do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

Our data strategy is strictly aligned with Rwanda's NST2 and Vision 2050 to build a transparent, data-driven green economy.

02 Data We Collect

Material Processing

Visual Data

Images uploaded for AI classification are processed to identify materials. We use anonymized circuit boards, batteries, and wires to retrain our neural networks.

Sensor Monitoring

IoT Telemetry

If using an EcoLoop Smart-Bin, we monitor VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) levels and fill-status to prevent toxic leaks and optimize pickup routes.

Marketplace Logic

Financial Data

Necessary for processing "Waste Worth" payouts. We collect digital wallet addresses to facilitate instant transfers during certified pickups.

Safety Logs

Compliance

We log interactions with safety prompts (Lead/Acid handling) to ensure user compliance with hazardous waste regulations.

03 The Don'ts

!

No Personal Identifiers

Don't upload images containing faces, personal documents, or sensitive ID cards. Our AI is trained only for waste recognition.

!

No Data Scraping

Don't attempt to scrape or reverse-engineer our proprietary "Waste Worth" valuation database or neural models.

!

No Credential Sharing

Don't share your private keys or digital wallet credentials with anyone claiming to be part of the EcoLoop team.

04 User Rights

You own your waste, and you own your data. You may request the full deletion of your "Waste Worth" history, GPS logs, and account profile at any time via the EcoLoop dashboard.
